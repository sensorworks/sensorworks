Making of a Virtual Smart Fence
(By Chetan Dewan)

It is imperative to augment this physical barrier with smart electronics such that advanced warning of attempts to infiltrate are available in all kinds of environments. I have developed a system which is a Virtual Smart Fence. This fence uses both wired and wireless sensors suitably integrated through a Universal Customised Control unit which is fully configurable as per requirements both in hardware and software. The system can be put in Layers. All sensors and sensing systems are battery powered, Mains/Genr charged and integrate solar charger as an option. They give both Audio and Visual alarm on detection and also remotely. These systems can operate 24x7, are all weather (IP55, 66, 67, 68 compliant based on type of enclosure selected), have CE, ROHS ratings and are about 500% cheaper than similar commercial systems.


There are basically three genre of system. All system can use any of the sensors provided on this website integrated to a Customised Universal Control Unit. The Architecture is based on a Guard Room which receives all sensors and then wirelessly Re-transmit status and video to the Incident
Command Post unit that can then receive multiple Guard Rooms Wirelessly for say a very large setup.


Some of the sensors which have been very successful are:-
(aa) Infrared Beam Barriers which can cover up to 250m per pair and can be put in tandem. These are both wired and wireless.
(ab) Proximity sensors based on Magnetic anomaly detection
(ac) Short range radars
(ad) PIR and Magnetic Anomaly detector combination sensor to mitigate false alarm.
(ae) Wireless PIR sensor nodes with repeaters
(af) Multi point detection trip wires
(ag) Fence Tilt and Cut Alarm
(ah) Fence Vibration sensor
(aj) Proximity sensing Anti Climb Sensor
(ak) Touch sensor as anti-tamper device
(al) Electro Fence with Alarm and Cameras
(al) Pressure sensor based on Strain gauge
(ii) All of the above sensors are low cost, have very low rate of false alarms, failure rate is also low.
